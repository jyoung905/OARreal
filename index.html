Common questions

FAQ

  • Is this free? Yes. The review is completely free. There is no cost and no obligation at any stage.
  • Is Ontario Accident Review a law firm? No. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice or legal representation. This is a review service that helps connect people with appropriate resources.
  • What happens after I submit? Your submission is reviewed by a representative. If your situation fits our criteria, we'll contact you with plain-language guidance on what benefits, options, or next steps may apply.
  • Will I get bombarded with calls? No. We review before we follow up. We only reach out if your situation appears worth pursuing, and we respect your preferred contact method.
  • Do I need insurance documents or IDs? No. This first stage only requires the basics. No insurance details, no uploads, no sensitive documents.
  • Does submitting start a legal case? No. Submitting this form is only a review request. It does not create a lawyer-client relationship or any legal commitment.
  • Are there deadlines for Ontario accident claims? Yes, Ontario has limitation periods that can affect your options. A free review now helps you understand where things stand before time becomes an issue.
