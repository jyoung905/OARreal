# Ontario Accident Review — Brand Direction

## Selected brand
**Ontario Accident Review**

## Why this was selected
After reviewing the existing project notes, this is the best launch choice for a first public version.

### Strengths
- **Immediately clear**: a visitor understands the service category on first glance.
- **Professional and law-firm-adjacent**: it sounds serious without pretending to be a law firm name.
- **Broad enough for the real intake mix**: it comfortably covers accident benefits, LAT disputes, slip and fall, dog bite, occupiers' liability, and other Ontario injury matters.
- **Better launch fit than a more abstract brand**: for paid ads, SEO, and a short-budget test, clarity matters more than cleverness.
- **Compliance-friendly**: it supports careful wording like “review,” “may qualify,” and “request a call,” which is safer than aggressive compensation language.

## Why not the main alternative
### FirstLine Claim Review
This is the strongest premium alternative and could become a better long-term brand if the project expands into a broader, more polished marketing system.

For launch, though, it needs more explanatory copy to communicate what it does. That added ambiguity is not ideal for a first low-friction lead funnel.

## Refined positioning
**Ontario Accident Review** should be presented as:

> A professional Ontario accident and injury claim review front door for people seeking guidance after an accident, denied benefits, or a developing dispute.

Not as:
- a direct promise of legal representation
- a guarantee of compensation
- a generic affiliate lead site
- a loud “injury ad” brand

## Working descriptor
**Accident benefits, LAT, and injury claim review in Ontario**

## Tone and trust rules
- Calm, direct, professional
- Education-forward, not hype-driven
- Clear that a short intake starts the review process
- Clear that follow-up depends on fit and review
- Avoid exaggerated legal claims or urgency tactics

## Launch messaging principles
Use:
- Tell us what happened
- Start your review
- Request a call
- Find out whether your matter may qualify for review

Avoid:
- We guarantee results
- Maximum compensation
- You are entitled to a payout
- Instant approval / instant settlement language

Launch under **Ontario Accident Review** with a clean, restrained visual identity:
- deep navy
- slate / gray neutrals
- muted teal accent
- modern sans-serif typography
- understated “review” / “path” visual treatment rather than legal clichés
